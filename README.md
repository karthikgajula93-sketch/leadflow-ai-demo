## What This Project Does

LeadFlow AI is a vertical SaaS product demo that presents an AI automation system for coaching center admissions management.

The platform demonstrates how coaching institutes can automate:

- Student inquiry capture and instant response
- AI-based lead qualification and priority scoring
- Automated follow-up sequences
- CRM state management and analytics
- Real-time lead pipeline visibility

This frontend is the presentation layer for backend automation systems built in Demo 2 (Lead Follow-Up) and Demo 3 (AI Lead Qualification).

---

## Problem It Solves

Independent coaching centers in India — particularly NEET/JEE centers — lose 30-40% of student inquiries due to:

- Delayed responses to late-night WhatsApp inquiries
- No consistent follow-up process after initial contact
- Manual CRM management through paper registers and WhatsApp chats
- No visibility into which leads convert and why

LeadFlow AI demonstrates how AI automation eliminates these operational bottlenecks.

## Design Decisions

**Vertical specificity over generic positioning**
Built exclusively for NEET/JEE coaching centers — not generic "AI for businesses." Specificity builds trust with niche clients.

**Operational realism over SaaS aesthetics**
Workflow diagrams, CRM visuals, and process steps instead of abstract AI imagery. Coaching center owners respond to business tools, not technology showcases.

**Credibility without fake social proof**
Zero fabricated client numbers or testimonials. Credibility built through operational specificity and realistic use cases.
